About Us

关于我们

The co-founders are two Ph.D. candidates at UPenn with a blend of scientific depth and entrepreneurial drive. With backgrounds spanning computational materials science, chemical engineering, mathematics, and business, we operate at the intersection of academia and industry. Guided by a deep commitment to impact, we seek visionary industrial partnerships to transform frontier scientific breakthroughs into scalable solutions.

我们的联合创始人是宾夕法尼亚大学的两位博士候选人，兼具科研深度与创业精神，专业背景涵盖计算材料科学、化学工程、数学与商业。我们致力于在学术与产业之间架起桥梁，推动前沿科研成果落地为可规模化的现实解决方案，并积极寻求具有远见的产业合作伙伴，共创变革性影响。
